ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT:
=======================

The DJ Danny Hectic B application is committed to accessibility and WCAG 2.1
Level AA compliance. All interactive elements are keyboard accessible, all
images have descriptive alt text, all color-coded information has text
alternatives, and the application is fully compatible with screen readers
including NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.

Users can navigate with keyboard alone (Tab, Shift+Tab, Enter, Space, Escape),
jump to main content with a skip link, and adjust for reduced motion
preferences. All form inputs have associated labels and error messages are
clearly announced.
